3D printed maps

We aim to provide 3D printed maps of areas based off of photos you upload to our site. The maps will track where you took photos on a trip.

Learn More

Origin

Do you remember that trip you took with your friends last year? No, no you don't. You have those pictures sitting somewhere on your phone or a backup somewhere, where you never look at them. With recall3d, we can give you a physical memento linked to your photos to help you remember you experiences.

Learn More

Our full stack

We begin by taking in an album of geotagged photos that you upload to our site. From there we pull the exif data and find the locations where you took them. From that, we take a square area around them and generate an STL file of that area. We generate a unique QR code as well, and append that to the bottom.
